Originally trained as a computer engineer during my undergraduate studies, I later transitioned to theoretical and mathematical research. My current interest is on applying this theoretical knowledge to practical matters in quantum computing, helping to build bridges between disciplines and contributing to the development of quantum technologies with sights set on the future.
