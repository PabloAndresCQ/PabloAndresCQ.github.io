Originally trained as a computer engineer during my undergraduate studies, I later transitioned to theoretical and mathematical research. I completed my PhD in quantum computer science at the University of Edinburgh (2022) on the topic of control flow of quantum programs and their categorical semantics. Since then, I have been working at Quantinuum as an R&D scientist. For the last two years, I have been developing pytket-cutensornet, a library for the simulation of quantum circuits using tensor network methods on GPUs.
